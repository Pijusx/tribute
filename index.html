A Tribute to the Fast & Furious Star

Paul Walker (1973-2013), American actor and philanthropist

Biography

Paul William Walker IV was an American actor best known for his role as Brian O'Conner in the Fast & Furious franchise. He was also a philanthropist and marine biology enthusiast.

Timeline

  • 1973 - Born in Glendale, California
  • 1986 - Started acting in TV shows
  • 2001 - Starred in "The Fast and the Furious"
  • 2010 - Founded charity organization "Reach Out Worldwide"
  • 2013 - Passed away in a car accident at age 40
